Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Common FAQs addressed concisely for curious travellers.

How do I book a flight?
Search for your route, select your preferred flight and fare class, fill in passenger details, and complete secure payment to receive your instant e-ticket.
What information do I need to book?
You'll need your full name as on your passport, date of birth, contact details, and a valid payment method. International travel requires passport details.
Can I book without a passport?
International travel requires a valid passport, while domestic flights typically accept government-issued photo ID. Always check entry requirements before booking.
When should I book for the best deals?
For the best prices, book domestic flights 1–3 months ahead and international flights 2–6 months ahead. Mid-week departures tend to be significantly cheaper.
